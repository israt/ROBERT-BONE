PROFESSIONAL MAGICIAL & MIND READERS

Robert is a professional close up magician, mind reader and self-proclaimed 'Demonstrator of Extra-Sensory Curiosities'. He uses a combination of sleight of hand, memory feats, psychological reading and hypnotic suggestion to achieve entertainment that is both fun and engaging. His interactive style and unique sense of wit creates an exciting, fresh lively genre of entertainment - perfect for interacting with your guests and initiating converstation that will go on long after Robert has finished. Close Up Table Magician

Robert performs at weddings, corporate hospitality events, awards galas, trade shows and private parties across the UK (and even internationally).

The versatility of Robert's magic and mind reading means he can entertain whilst mingling with guests during a reception, between the dinner tables as well as a stand-up cabaret show.

Robert offers packages to fit all events and budgets of all sizes, such as reception drinks, around the tables and even after-dinner cabaret.

Private parties, wedding and corporate entertainment

Robert is a full-time professional mind-reader and magician, a member of the prestigious Magic Circle and Equity. You can rest assured that your guests will be in capable hands seeing some amazing entertainment. Whether your guests all know each other or are strangers and need something to break the ice, Robert's interactive performance (either on stage or close-up) is a real conversation starter.

Based in Berkshire, he has easy access to London, Home Counties and the South Coast , and entertains at events across the UK, as well as the occasional international event.

You can book Robert with confidence, having turned full-time professional in 2006 he has a wealth of experience and should the worst happen he has £10 million Public Liability Insurance. Basically, Robert isn't a hobbyist earning a few extra quid, but an experienced professional and therefore provides a top quality professional service.
